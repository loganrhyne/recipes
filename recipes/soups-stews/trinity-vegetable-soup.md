## Ingredients
- 1 celery stalk, washed and sliced
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- 6 carrots, scrubbed and sliced
- 3 potatoes, scrubbed and cubed
- 1/2 pound green beans, washed and cut
- 1/2 pound fresh okra, washed and sliced, or 1 (10-ounce) package frozen okra
- 1 (48-ounce) can V-8 juice
- 1 (10 1/2-ounce) can beef bouillon
- 1 (16-ounce) can tomatoes or 2 fresh tomatoes, chopped
- 1 (16-ounce) can corn or 2 cups fresh corn
- 1 (16-ounce) can peas or 2 cups shelled fresh peas
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
- 1 teaspoon Worcestershire sauce

## Instructions
1. Prepare the vegetables and set aside.
2. In a large pot, combine V-8 juice and beef bouillon; bring to a boil.
3. Add fresh vegetables and return to a boil. If using fresh tomatoes, corn, and peas, add 1 cup water.
4. Cook uncovered for 15 minutes.
5. Add undrained canned vegetables and seasonings. Cover and simmer for 30 minutes, or until vegetables are tender.
6. Serve in bowls or mugs with crackers.

## Notes & Tips
- First grade students at Trinity School in Atlanta make this soup every year; each child brings a different vegetable.
