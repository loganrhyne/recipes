## Ingredients
- 1/2 pound salt pork, minced
- 2 pounds beef stew meat, trimmed and cut into bite-sized pieces
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 1 beef bouillon cube
- 1 garlic clove, crushed (optional)
- 1 cup water
- 1 cup cooked carrots
- 1 cup uncooked peas
- Salt and pepper, to taste
- Chopped parsley

## Instructions
1. In a heavy pot, sauté salt pork until browned. Remove and reserve.
2. Pour off excess fat, leaving 2 to 3 tablespoons in the pot. Brown the beef well.
3. Sprinkle with flour and cook a few minutes longer.
4. Add the onion, bouillon cube, garlic (if using), and water.
5. Cover and simmer on the stovetop or bake in a preheated 325°F oven for 1 hour, or until beef is tender.
6. Add carrots, peas, salt, and pepper; bake 30 minutes longer.
7. Garnish with parsley and serve.

## Notes & Tips
- Some fried pork pieces may be added to the stew before simmering or baking, if desired.

## Storage
- Can be frozen.
